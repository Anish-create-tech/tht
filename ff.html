Core Product Features

01

Dynamic Phase-Change Microchannels

Microfluidic channels contain tailored PCMs that can be actively circulated. The flow path and rate are controlled to shift latent heat from rooftop hotspots to cooler zones or to dumps for optimized thermal smoothing.

02

Integrated Thin-Film Thermoelectric Generators (TEGs)

High-efficiency thin-film TEG arrays harvest millivolt differentials between the reflective surface and PCM layers, producing DC power to run local sensors, micro-fans, or export to building microgrids.

03

Self-Cleaning Nano-Reflective Surface

Nano-textured hydrophobic coating combined with scheduled micro-vibration pulses reduces dust accumulation and preserves high albedo throughout seasons with minimal manual maintenance.

04

Modular, Swappable Pod Design

Pods are ~1 m² modules with plug-and-play mechanical mounts and electrical connectors. Swap or replace pods for upgrades, maintenance, or redeployment—ideal for phased rollouts and pilot programs.

05

AI Thermal Mapping & Control

Distributed thermal sensors feed a lightweight edge AI that produces rooftop heat maps. The AI optimizes PCM flow, cleaning cycles, and TEG operating points to maximize cooling and energy yield.

06

Edge Computing & Analytics (SaaS)

On-device controllers run analytics and optionally sync aggregated metrics to a cloud dashboard: energy harvested (kWh), peak temp reduction, maintenance alerts, lifecycle predictions.

07

Installation & Maintenance Features

Quick-mount clamps, standardized electrical plugs (DC), modular PCM cartridges for safe swaps, and self-test diagnostics for flow, TEG health, and surface cleanliness.
